Blockchain @ BITS
===================================

The main goals of society are:

1. To create awareness on the technical and fundamental concepts of Blockchain
technology amongst students and faculty members.

2. To educate on the development of the Distributed applications, Distributed autonomous
organizations (DAOs), Tokens, Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs), etc. on various platforms
including Ethereum, Hyperledger, Solana, Binance, Algorand, Polkadot, etc. The society
shall organize hackathons, knowledge sharing sessions, and other activities for
knowledge sharing and education.

3. To create an environment for discussion on the ever-emerging field of cryptocurrencies,
understand the correctness of various cryptocurrencies and the importance of systematic
investments.

4. To create a culture where students and faculty members will create meaningful practical
projects and products. Society will share the knowledge of product development from
ideation to development to taking the same to the market.

5. The society will raise funding on its own for organizing events, training and execution of
its goals.
